School Days Out at Borth Zoo, Wales

School Visits

School visits can be booked by telephone, and a special talk and demonstration can be offered with 'hands on' snake, lizard and rabbit (Depending on the age and interest of the children).  Various animals may be included and times can be adjusted to suit.  This talk is entertaining and educational

Party rate applies with one free teacher for every ten children.

Schools visit the Animalarium for a fun day out, but we also offer an educational experience which can tie in with national curriculum e.g.Biology, Nature Study, Art, Science and Creative Writing.  We can supply work sheets to suit different age groups. A special low price can be arranged for schools that intend to visit for educational reasons.

For further information and to book your school party at the Animalarium please telephone: 01970 871224

Work Experience

Schoolchildren and College students may come to the Animalarium in Wales  for their work experience, or for part of their Animal Carer courses. We train the students to care for the animals here, and to study animal welfare issues.  The work experience can be just for one week or can be extended and form part of the course.

University students attend as part of their Biology course and to conduct behavioural experiments, theses and dissertations.
