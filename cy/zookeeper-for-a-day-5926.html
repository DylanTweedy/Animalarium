Want to be a 'Zookeeper for a Day'?

Have you ever wanted to be a Zookeeper and to get close to the animals and inter-act with them as a zookeeper does? 

We offer a day long experience when you may follow a keeper as they look after the animals.  You may enter the enclosures the same as a keeper.  You may choose which animals you look after and have the same amount of contact as the keeper does, depending on the animals you choose and having due regard to your safety.

The experience is from 10.30 to 3.30 with an hour off for lunch.

The price is £80.00 per person, any age.  A child under nine years old must be accompanied by an adult, both will be charged.

Contact us to make a booking.
